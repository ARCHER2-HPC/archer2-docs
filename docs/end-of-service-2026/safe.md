# Personal data on SAFE at end of service

A full description of how we handle personal data on SAFE can be 
found in the
[SAFE Personal Data and Privacy Policy](https://www.archer2.ac.uk/about/policies/safe_privacy_policy.html).
A summary of impacts for users and projects on ARCHER2 that are active at the end of the service:

- We will retain your personal details for a period of 2 years to allow
  you and the research councils to access historic data on the use of the
  ARCHER2 service.
- After 2 years, all data will be anonymised and your personal details
  deleted from the SAFE database.
- All email records to the ARCHER2 service desk stored in the SAFE database
  will be deleted 2 years after the end of service.

If you wish for us to remove your personal details from the SAFE sooner than 
the 2 year deadline after the service ends, you can do this by contacting the
ARCHER2 support address (support@archer2.ac.uk), this email address will
continue to be monitored after the end of the ARCHER2 service.
